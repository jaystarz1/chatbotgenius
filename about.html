About Jay Tarzwell

The Chatbot Genius

AI & Privacy Expert | Military Veteran | Author

As an AI consultant and military veteran, I build bespoke apps and design high-impact prompts, with a relentless discipline to ensure these tools deliver significant, practical results.

What does this look like in practice? It means dedicated, iterative development, like the 14 months I spent evolving a ChatGPT prompt into the webapp medictate.ai. This tool now supports physicians by transforming voice notes into formatted reports, enabling them to focus on patient care.

My work frequently involves Personally Identifiable Information (PII) and other sensitive data, particularly in the medical and HR fields. This hands-on experience has driven me to make robust data privacy and information security foundational to my AI solutions. For clients, especially in legal and medical practices where privacy is paramount and often governed by strict jurisdictional rules, I specialize in understanding their unique privacy landscape and proactively implementing solutions to ensure compliance.

My core strategy involves meticulous de-identification of PII, enabling data to be processed by commercially available AI models without compromising information security. To empower my clients and the broader community, I actively research and create resources, including publishing white papers on personal data security practices across the English-speaking world.
