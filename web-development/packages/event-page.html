Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I get my event page?

▼

We deliver your completed event page within 24 hours of receiving your information and payment. For Premier packages with SEO, we may take up to 48 hours to ensure proper optimization.

Can I make changes after the page is live?

▼

Yes! Basic changes are $15 each through our pay-as-you-go service. Premier packages include 1 update per month. For recurring events, we offer update packages at discounted rates.

How long will my event page stay online?

▼

Basic event pages include hosting for 14 days after your event date. For longer hosting, choose our Recurring ($6/month) or Premier (first year included) packages.

Do I need to provide content and images?

▼

Just provide basic event details - our AI creates all the content! If you have specific images or logos you'd like included, we're happy to use them. Otherwise, we'll create or source appropriate visuals.

Can guests RSVP directly on the page?

▼

Yes! Every event page includes a customizable RSVP or contact form. Responses are sent directly to your email address.
