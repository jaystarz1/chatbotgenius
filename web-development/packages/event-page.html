Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can my event page be ready?

Basic event pages are delivered within 24 hours of our initial call. Premier event pages with SEO optimization take 48 hours. If you need it even faster, we offer rush delivery for an additional $50.

What's the difference between private and SEO-optimized pages?

Basic and Recurring event pages are intentionally NOT indexed by Google - perfect for private events. Premier pages include full SEO optimization, making them discoverable in search results - ideal for public events, fundraisers, and conferences.

Can I make changes after the page is live?

Yes! Minor text changes are $15 each. Major updates (new sections, design changes) are $47. Premier packages include 1 update per month free.

Do I need to provide hosting?

No! Basic pages include hosting until 14 days after your event. Recurring and Premier pages include hosting (Recurring is $6/month after the first month, Premier is $10/month after the first year).

What if I want to keep my event page as a memory?

We offer a Memory Archive service for $25/year that keeps your event page online indefinitely. Perfect for weddings and milestone celebrations!
