Check out our Standard Package (10 pages for $497) or Complete Package (25 pages for $997). You can also start with the Landing Package and add pages as you grow.

Do I need to provide content?

Yes, you provide the text content and images. However, we can help structure and optimize your content for SEO. If you need professional copywriting, we can refer you to our partners.

Can I update the site myself?

Yes! We can teach you to make updates using AI tools (see our DIY AI Training for $497), or you can use our maintenance services starting at $15 per update.

What about hosting?

We set up your site on free hosting platforms like GitHub Pages or Netlify. You only pay for your domain name (about $20/year). No monthly hosting fees!

Is this good for local SEO?

Absolutely! We include local SEO optimization, Google Business integration, and schema markup to help you rank in local searches. Perfect for Ottawa businesses!
