Technical Workshops & Labs

Hands-on learning experiences with industry experts (Day 2)

Deep dive into cutting-edge AI technologies with our intensive workshop program. Each session is limited to 30 participants to ensure personalized attention and hands-on learning.

Morning Block A (9:00 AM - 12:00 PM)

MLOps & Model Deployment

Intermediate 30 spots

Led by: Alex Chen, Senior ML Engineer, Shopify

Learn to deploy, monitor, and maintain machine learning models in production environments.

What You'll Learn:
  • Model versioning and deployment strategies
  • Monitoring model performance and drift
  • CI/CD pipelines for ML workflows
  • Docker containerization for models
  • A/B testing ML models in production
Prerequisites:

Python experience, basic ML knowledge, laptop required

Computer Vision Applications

Intermediate 30 spots

Led by: Dr. Priya Sharma, Microsoft Canada

Build real-world computer vision applications using state-of-the-art deep learning techniques.

What You'll Learn:
  • Object detection and image classification
  • Transfer learning with pre-trained models
  • Data augmentation techniques
  • Real-time video processing
  • Deployment with Azure Cognitive Services
Prerequisites:

Python, basic neural network concepts, laptop with GPU preferred

Natural Language Processing

Beginner 30 spots

Led by: Dr. Maria Santos, NLP Research Lead, Cohere

Master the fundamentals of NLP from text preprocessing to advanced language models.

What You'll Learn:
  • Text preprocessing and tokenization
  • Sentiment analysis and classification
  • Named entity recognition (NER)
  • Working with transformer models
  • Building chatbots and Q&A systems
Prerequisites:

Basic Python knowledge, no prior NLP experience required

Afternoon Block B (1:00 PM - 4:00 PM)

Generative AI & Large Language Models

Advanced 30 spots

Led by: Michael O'Brien, Founder & CEO, Cohere

Explore the latest in generative AI, from GPT models to business applications.

What You'll Learn:
  • Understanding transformer architecture
  • Fine-tuning large language models
  • Prompt engineering best practices
  • Building LLM-powered applications
  • Ethical considerations in generative AI
Prerequisites:

Strong ML background, experience with PyTorch/TensorFlow

AI Ethics Implementation

All Levels 30 spots

Led by: Dr. Rachel Anderson, CIFAR AI & Society

Learn practical approaches to implementing ethical AI in your organization.

What You'll Learn:
  • Bias detection and mitigation strategies
  • Fairness metrics and evaluation
  • Explainable AI techniques
  • Privacy-preserving ML methods
  • Building ethical review processes
Prerequisites:

Basic understanding of AI/ML concepts, no coding required

Reinforcement Learning

Advanced 30 spots

Led by: Dr. Jennifer Park, Vector Institute

Master the principles of reinforcement learning and its real-world applications.

What You'll Learn:
  • Q-learning and policy gradient methods
  • Deep reinforcement learning with PyTorch
  • Multi-agent reinforcement learning
  • Applications in robotics and gaming
  • Hyperparameter tuning and optimization
Prerequisites:

Strong Python and ML background, calculus and linear algebra

Getting There

🚗 By Car

Paid parking available on-site and nearby. Street parking is limited in downtown Ottawa.

🚌 Public Transit (OC Transpo)

Take the O-Train to Rideau Station, then connect to bus routes 7, 14, or 18 to Colonel By/Laurier stop.

Ottawa Macdonald-Cartier International Airport is 20 minutes by car or taxi. Airport shuttle services available.

Downtown hotels are within 10-minute walk. Rideau Centre shopping mall is 5 minutes away.
