Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our web development service

How can you build websites so quickly?

▼

We use AI-powered tools to accelerate the design and development process. Our AI handles the heavy lifting of code generation, content writing, and design creation, while our human experts ensure quality and customization. This combination allows us to deliver in days what traditional agencies take weeks to complete.

What about monthly hosting fees?

▼

We use modern hosting solutions like GitHub Pages or Netlify that offer free hosting for static websites. You only pay for your domain name (about $20/year). There are no monthly hosting fees, no maintenance contracts, and no hidden costs. You own everything.

Can I update the website myself?

▼

Yes! We offer three options: 1) Our DIY AI Training package ($497) teaches you to use AI tools to update your own site, 2) Monthly monitoring service ($97/month) where we handle all updates, or 3) Pay-as-you-go updates ($15 each). You choose what works best for you.

Do you work with businesses outside Ottawa?

▼

While we specialize in Ottawa businesses and local SEO, we welcome clients from anywhere in Canada. Our process is entirely remote-friendly, with video calls for consultation and online collaboration throughout the project.

What if I need changes after launch?

▼

Every package includes a period of free updates (1-3 months depending on the package). After that, you can choose our monthly monitoring service, pay-as-you-go updates, or learn to do it yourself with our AI training. We never lock you in.
