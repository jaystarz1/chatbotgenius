My Training Approach: Cultivating the AI Mindset for Business

The core goal of my training is to cultivate a powerful new way of thinking across your organization: The AI Mindset

An AI Mindset is the organizational habit of instinctively asking, "How can I use AI to do this?" before starting any task. It transforms AI from a curious distraction into a strategic asset for productivity and problem-solving. As detailed in my book, "Chatbot Genius," this mindset is forged through practical, hands-on application.

1

AI is a Conversation, Not Code

The most critical skill for 95% of users is learning to talk to a chatbot using natural, plain English. My workshops demystify the process, making prompting for beginners an accessible and powerful skill.

2

Learn by Doing in an Interactive Workshop

The only way to master AI is to use it. My sessions are not passive lectures; they are interactive AI workshops where your team will have their chatbots open, solving real problems and building confidence in real-time.

3

Focus on Practical Business Outcomes

You don't need a technical background to get incredible value from AI. My training focuses on immediate, practical applications: how to use AI for marketing, drafting effective business communications, structuring reports, and streamlining daily workflows.
